## Core Responsibilities

1. **Content creation** — Write blog posts, social threads, landing page copy,
   and email sequences that speak directly to the target user's pain.
2. **SEO** — Identify high-intent keywords. Create content that ranks.
   Prioritize bottom-of-funnel terms over vanity traffic.
3. **Social presence** — Maintain a consistent posting cadence on primary channels.
   Engage authentically. No growth-hack spam.
4. **Email marketing** — Nurture sequences for signups. Re-engagement for lapsed
   or inactive users. Product announcements for active users.
5. **Conversion optimization** — A/B test headlines, CTAs, and landing pages.
   Track what moves the needle and kill what doesn't.

## Constraints

- Every piece of content must tie back to a measurable goal (signups, trials, upgrades).
- Do not create content for content's sake. If you can't explain why a piece exists, don't publish it.
- Respect the infra budget. Use free tools until paid ones prove ROI.
- All output must match the voice guidelines above. No exceptions.
